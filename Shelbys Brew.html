    Coffee Drinks

  • TYPES OF WAYS TO DRINK
    • Americano: Espresso shots on water
    • Latte:Espresso shots on milk
    • Mocha: Latte with Chocolate Flavor
    • Cappucino: Very Foamy typically short latte, served wet or dry (more milk, less milk)
    • Cortado: 2 oz of steamed milk and 2 oz of espresso (mini latte)
    • Traditional Macchiato: espressso shots and a dollop of foam

      • FLAVOR GUIDE
        • Ordering fruit flavors in coffee? I reccommend berry flavors with chocolate!
        • If your looking for a nuttier taste try using almond milk or adding flavors like hazelnut.
        • Looking for subtle flavor vanilla and caramel are always great basics.
        • Looking for a floral taste try lavender or violet (ask for a smaller amount for your first try)
        • Authors Favorites: Oatmilk latte with caramel and/or vanilla flavor

    • No Caffeine Drinks

    Check if the shop has smoothie options or decaf coffee. Another great option is a Chai Latte! Ask if they have herbal decaf tea.

    Authors Favorite: I love a good chai latte with oat milk or a refreshing mango or strawberry smoothie!

    Tea Drinks

    Check at the store which teas are caffeinated and not! Herbal tea typically is not caffeinated.

    • Matcha is the highest caffenated tea, then comes black tea then green tea.
    • When ordering black tea try pairing it with strawberry or lemonade!
    • When ordering green tea try pairing with a honey or peach flavor!
    • With matcha go for a latte style and try adding vanilla or lavender!
    • Authors Favorite: Black tea with splash of lemonade, Vanilla Matcha with oatmilk, Peach green tea with lemonade
